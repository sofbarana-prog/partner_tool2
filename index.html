ⓘ Note on co-financing

Suggested calls may be used as co-financing or sequential funding sources, subject to the rule that the same cost items cannot be covered twice by different programmes (Art. 15, Reg. EU 2021/695). Each suggested call includes a note on its cumulation relationship with Horizon Europe.
